The Rural Initiative Plan

InsightIQ offers it's unique Rural Initiative Plan for the underprivileged educational institutes.. Educational support and resources will be offered to people or communities where one is unable to host a developed educational platform. One can contact InsightIQ with relevant credentials and after a short verification, our members will reach out to you providing the necessary support.


View Larger Map

How We Help:

1. We verify your information regarding educational quality around your residence.

2. If the educational institutes lacks modern equipments and is in need of one to make the learning experience easier, our team will try to provide one.

3. We might also provide study materials to instituions that are in need of such.

We contact further NGOs for collaborating with us regarding bigger projects.
